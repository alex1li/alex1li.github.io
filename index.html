Skywriter Lightsaber

A senior capstone project for my ECE major. We built a lightsaber with a twist - persistence of vision. This lightsaber can write words and draw images in the air as it's waved. Designed from scratch. Built with tape.

Presidential Tweet Classifier

A machine learning web app that predicts if a tweet from the account @realDonaldTrump is written by Donald Trump or a member of his staff. I co-developed the project with a my friend Rohan Patel and collaborated with CS professors and graduate students. I never want to read another Trump tweet but we achieved 90% testing accuracy.

Energy and the Environment Lab

Cornell's Energy and the Environment Lab does community-focused environmental research. I focused on how biomass burning emissions, like wood smoke, can be more effectively monitored. Be careful how you burn wood, or you can damage your health and accelerate global warming! I presented at the American Association for Aerosol Research Conference.

Conference Presentation

Cornell Rocketry Team

CRT is an awesome group of people that design and build launch vehicles for a competition hosted by NASA. On the team, I built the parachute landing system to prevent the rocket from smashing to pieces as it comes down. And I built LoRa radios to help the team find the rocket after it lands.

Filmmaking

I work with a small team of friends to write, shoot, and edit short movies. We love making movies that advocate for important causes. In the past, our films have become TV commercials and have been presented at the National Academy of Engineering.
