ALEX LI

Alex is a researcher and engineer whose work explores how we build a sustainable future through technology innovation and global collaboration.

Currently based in China, Alex works at Envision Energy as a product manager on industrial decarbonization solutions. He is also the co-founder of the U.S.-China Climate Forum, organizing Track 2 dialogues for climate collaboration.

He graduated from Tsinghua’s Schwarzman Scholars program in 2022 and studied electrical and computer engineering at Cornell. He previously worked at McKinsey serving clients in the U.S. renewables and electric vehicle industries.
